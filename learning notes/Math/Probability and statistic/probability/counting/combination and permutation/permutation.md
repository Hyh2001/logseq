Permutation is defined as follow. A permutation of $n$ distinct elements taken $r$ at a time is an arrangement of the $r$ elements. ==It is a [[counting]] that the order matters.== Its calculation formula is represented as follow.
# calculation formula 
$P^{n}_{r} = \frac{n!}{(n-r)!}$
