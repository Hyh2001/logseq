The marginal probability distribution of $X$ alone and $Y$ alone are $p_x(x) = P(X=x) = P(\cup_j \{X=x,Y=y\}) = \sum_jP(X=x,Y=y_j)=\sum_jp(x,y_j)$or $p_x(x) = \int^{+\infty}_{-\infty}p(x,y)dy$
$p_y(y) = \sum_ip(x_i,y)$  or $p_y(y) = \int^{+\infty}_{-\infty}p(x,y)dx$
==use [[Baye‘s rule]] , we can see the result.==
