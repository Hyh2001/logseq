# operations:
## adding vectors
$a + b$
## multiply by scalar
$c \cdot a$
## [[dot product]]
$v \cdot w = v_1w_1 + v_2w_2$
## lengths
$||v|| = \sqrt[]{v \cdot v}=\sqrt[]{v_1^2+v_2^2+...+v_n^2}$
## angles 
$u \cdot v /||u||||v||= cos \theta$
### properties
$|v \cdot w| \leq ||v||||w||$
$||v + w|| \leq ||v|| + ||w||$
